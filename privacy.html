Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

DealorDudd ("we", "us", "our") operates dealordudd.com and provides AI-powered deal analysis and SMS/email deal alerts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info: name, email address, mobile phone number you provide at signup.
  • Payment info: processed by Stripe; we do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage data: deal queries, click history, and engagement metrics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Send recurring SMS deal alerts and email digests you opted into.
  • Send transactional notifications (signup confirmation, payment receipts, password resets).
  • Improve our AI deal-verdict accuracy.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. SMS / Text Messaging

By providing your mobile number and opting in, you agree to receive recurring SMS messages from DealorDudd. Message frequency is up to 1 message per day. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to cancel, or HELP for help. We do not share or sell your mobile number, opt-in data, or consent to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing. We share data only with: (a) service providers who help us operate (Twilio for SMS, Stripe for payments, Brevo/Gmail for email), and (b) when required by law.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your account information by emailing support@dealordudd.com. You can opt out of SMS by replying STOP, and out of email by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email.

7. Children

DealorDudd is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

9. Contact

Questions: support@dealordudd.com
